About Tidewater Chapter

Tidewater Chapter, Order of DeMolay, is a vibrant youth organization serving young men ages 12–21 in the Tidewater / Hampton Roads area of Virginia. We are part of DeMolay International – the world's premier youth organization for young men – with a heritage dating back to 1919.

🌟

Our Mission

To develop confident, capable, and civic-minded young men through a program centered on seven cardinal virtues and meaningful leadership experience.

🤝

Brotherhood

Members form lifelong bonds of friendship and mutual support, learning to work together toward shared goals.

🌎

Community Service

From local fundraisers to regional volunteer projects, we give back to the communities that support us.

Why DeMolay?

DeMolay International has helped shape generations of leaders. Our alumni include a US President, astronauts, governors, and Fortune 500 executives – but every success story starts right here, in a local chapter like ours.
